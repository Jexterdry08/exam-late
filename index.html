What is STAGES?


Life puts in front of us a primary confusion that stops us in our tracks--

Who have I been?
Who am I now?
Who am I becoming?

These questions make us slow down until we solve the developmental confusions we are facing

How can we get feedback on
our healthy contradictions,
and discover the new version of who we are?

STAGES Online Courses

The Three Questions

Discovering Who You Are

To start on your development journey, we only have to respond to three questions so that we can discover and navigate these healthy confusions. Our first course will help you dive deeply into your present identity. However, we know that we constantly change, so we can continue to ask these three questions throughout our lives to discover who the "new me" is.

Developmental Confusions

The second course in this series "Developmental Confusions" will describe the primary confusion at each development stage that we must solve in order to continue into our emerging identity.






Mind's I

12 Month Developmental Journey

The third course in this series "The Mind's I" will lead us through the journey of our life's path in practical ways. It combines the past, present, and future identities we navigate through and confronts the healthy confusion we meet and overcome at each stage.
